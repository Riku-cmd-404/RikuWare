🔧 Injection Guide (Extreme Injector + Manual Map)

Follow these steps carefully to ensure proper injection:

  • Step 1: Download Extreme Injector v3.7.3+ from github.com/master131/ExtremeInjector or trusted source.
  • Step 2: Run Extreme Injector.exe as Administrator (right-click → Run as administrator).
  • Step 3: In the injector, select Counter-Strike 2 from the process list (cs2.exe).
  • Step 4: Click Add DLL and choose the downloaded RikuWare.dll.
  • Step 5: Under injection settings, set Injection Method to Manual Map.
  • Step 6: Click Inject — wait for confirmation sound/notification.
  • Step 7: Launch CS2 (if not already open) and press INSERT or RIGHT SHIFT to open cheat menu.

⚠️ Important: Always use Manual Map injection mode. Standard injection may be detected. Run injector as administrator and disable antivirus temporarily if injection fails (false positives are common).

🛠️ Troubleshooting

  • Injection fails? Make sure Extreme Injector is running as admin, and CS2 is not running with higher integrity. Try restarting both.
  • Menu doesn't open? Press INSERT or RIGHT SHIFT. If nothing happens, injection might have failed. Re-inject using Manual Map.
  • Antivirus deletes RikuWare.dll? Add an exception to your download folder or temporarily disable real-time protection (false positive due to packing).
  • Game crashes after injection? Try different Manual Map settings (e.g., "Manual Map (No Stealth)" or "Manual Map (Extreme)".
  • VAC warning? Cheat is not guaranteed undetected. Use on alternate accounts only.
